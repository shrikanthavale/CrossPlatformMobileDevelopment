Help

How to play

Firstly, a set of cards is presented on a grid. You have a few seconds to memorize the location each card until they are rotated to face down. Your goal is to pair together cards with the same image.

Predefined and Custom Games

You can choose among a predefined variety of card categories (e.g. animals, trees, shapes) or simply create your own custom cards using your camera or Facebook photos.

Check your scores

After game session, don't forget to check your score and share it on Facebook.
